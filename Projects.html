SkillTracker - Job Analytics

SkillTracker-Job Analytics is a web application that allows users to search for job opportunities based on their skills. The app provides a comprehensive list of available jobs based on the user's search criteria and allows them to apply directly from the platform. The app also features basic analytics on the job market, providing insights on job trends and in-demand skills.
